DI-LAB

The Yonsei Design & Intelligence Research Lab

Research Area

We take human-centered approach to ignite innovative thinkings!

DI-lab is a human-computer interaction research group in the Graduate School of Communication and Arts at Yonsei University. In our lab, we are taking a human-centered approach to conduct interdisciplinary research that solves problems in various domains including technology, design, and social science. Our major research interest is to apply computational skills and methods to the process of making and distributing information. Stay tuned for our research updates!

Computational Journalism

We explore various media-related technologies that can add value to the process of making and distributing news to people.

Human-AI Interaction

What will change as AI permeates deeper into our lives? Let's prepare a world where humans make daily interactions with AI.

Social Computing

Computing is fundamentally a social activity. Innovative thinking across many disciplines is required to deal with such environment.

User Experience Design

We conduct studies using various UX and service design methods to scrutinize the users' needs and wants in the process. Join us!.
